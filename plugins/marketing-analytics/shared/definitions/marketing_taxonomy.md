# Marketing Taxonomy

Standard definitions and terminology used across all marketing analytics skills.

## Channel Taxonomy

| Channel | Sub-channels | Primary Metrics |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Paid Search | Google Ads, Microsoft Ads | CPC, CPA, ROAS, Quality Score |
| Paid Social | Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok | CPM, CPA, ROAS, Engagement Rate |
| Display | DV360, Google Display | CPM, Viewability, CPA |
| Organic Search | Google, Bing | Rankings, CTR, Traffic, Conversions |
| Email | Campaigns, Lifecycle Flows | CTDR, Revenue/Email, List Health |
| Social (Organic) | Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, X | Engagement Rate, Reach, SOV |
| Direct | Type-in, Bookmarks | Sessions, Conversion Rate |
| Referral | Partner sites, PR | Referral Traffic, Conversion Rate |

## Metric Glossary

| Metric | Definition | Formula |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| ROAS | Return on ad spend | Revenue / Spend |
| CPA | Cost per acquisition | Spend / Conversions |
| CPC | Cost per click | Spend / Clicks |
| CPM | Cost per mille | (Spend / Impressions) * 1000 |
| CTR | Click-through rate | Clicks / Impressions |
| CTDR | Click-to-delivered rate | Clicks / Delivered |
| CVR | Conversion rate | Conversions / Sessions (or Clicks) |
| CLV | Customer lifetime value | E[transactions] * E[monetary] * margin |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score | %Promoters - %Detractors |
| CSAT | Customer satisfaction | %Satisfied / %Respondents |
| CES | Customer effort score | Mean(effort_ratings) |
| SOV | Share of voice | Brand mentions / Total category mentions |
| MDE | Minimum detectable effect | Smallest effect an experiment can reliably detect |

## Segment Labels (RFM)

| Segment | RFM Pattern | Description |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| Champions | High R, High F, High M | Best customers, bought recently, buy often, spend the most |
| Loyal | Mid-High R, High F | Buy regularly, responsive to promotions |
| Potential Loyalists | High R, Mid F | Recent customers with potential for higher frequency |
| New Customers | High R, Low F | Bought recently but not frequently |
| Promising | Mid R, Mid F | Average engagement, room for growth |
| Need Attention | Mid R, Mid F, Mid M | Above average but trending downward |
| About to Sleep | Low-Mid R, Low-Mid F | Below average, at risk of churning |
| At Risk | Low R, High F | Used to buy frequently but haven't recently |
| Hibernating | Low R, Low F | Last purchase was long ago, low frequency |
| Lost | Very Low R, Low F | Haven't purchased in a very long time |
